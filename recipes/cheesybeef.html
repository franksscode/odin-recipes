Cheesy Beef Pasta

How to make cheesy beef pasta

This is a super simple, one pot recipe. You just need to boil the water, cook the pasta drain the pasta, cook the beef, add the pasta to the same pot, add the sauce and cheese as well and mix until combined and cheese melts!

Ingredients

  • 56g Elbow pasta(or your favorite)
  • 120 ml Prego Mushroom Sauce(or your favorite)
  • 150g of lean ground beef(just get the leanest you can find)
  • 56g of Kraft low moisture shredded mozzarella(or what you can find)

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Cook pasta for 6 minutes(al dente pasta is GOOD)
  3. Cook your beef until browned(you can season it with what you like first)
  4. After rinsing pasta, add it to the beef in the same pot
  5. Add the cheese and sauce
  6. Turn the heat back on and mix until combined and cheese is melted
  7. Enjoy! :)
