How To Make a Pizza

To make a pizza from scratch, start by preparing the dough. Combine flour, yeast, salt, and water, then knead the dough until smooth and let it rise for about an hour. While the dough rises, make your sauce by blending tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and seasonings. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and roll it out to your desired thickness. Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 360ml water
  • 7g yeast
  • 30ml olive il
  • 5g salt
  • 5g sugar

For the Sauce

  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 15ml olive oil
  • 1g dried oregano
  • 5g Salt
  • 1g Black Pepper
  • A pinch of red pepper flakes

Toppings

  • 200g shredded mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 36g of turkey pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make the Dough:
    • In a bowl, combine 360ml warm water, 7g active dry yeast, and 5g sugar (optional). Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • In a separate large bowl, mix 440g flour and 5g salt.
    • Add the yeast mixture and 30ml olive oil to the flour mixture. Stir until the dough begins to form.
    • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • While the dough is rising, heat 15ml olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
    • Add 2 cloves of minced garlic (6g) and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add 400g crushed tomatoes, 1g dried oregano, 5g salt, 1g black pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes (optional). Stir to combine.
    • Simmer the sauce over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C) for at least 30 minutes before baking.
  4. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the risen dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
    • Roll it out to your desired thickness, about 12 inches in diameter for a standard pizza.
    • Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
    • Sprinkle 200g shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    • Add your favorite toppings (e.g., 50-100g of pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.).
  6. Bake the Pizza:
    • Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Finish and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    • Optionally, add fresh basil leaves on top and drizzle with a little olive oil.
    • Slice, serve, and enjoy!
