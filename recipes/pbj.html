PBJ Sandwhich

How to make PBJ

Grab your favorite bread, jelly, and peanut butter and slap it together. Like it warm? Toast it! Cold? Make it and refrigerate for a few hours. You could also just cut the crust, slice the sandwhich into a circle shape, and pinch the sides to make a homemade Crustable!

Ingredients

  • Bread(2 slices)
  • Peanut Butter(the amount your heart tells you)
  • Jelly(flavor and amount your ancestors whisper to you)
  • Love

Steps

  1. Grab a slice of bread
  2. Slap peanut butter on it
  3. Grab the other slice
  4. Slap jelly on it
  5. Put the slices together
  6. In the words of those IG influencers, "And love your life!"
